Welcome To A3S Solutions

A3S Solutions, was established in year 2010 as Distribution/Trading Company specialized in Industrial AC & DC Systems such as Un-Interrupted Power Supply System (UPS), Rectifiers/Chargers, Batteries, Manufactured by AEG Power Solutions conversion d’energie.

Today A3S Solutions is a full fledge staging facility for various control system cabinets and a strong reliable distributor / Channel Partner with local Competence Centre for AEG Power Solutions AC & DC Systems, FreeWave Technologies Wireless Solutions, Systems SUNLIGHT Lead Acid Batteries, Trina Solar Solar Panels and The MICHA for Solar Charge Controllers having offices in Dubai, UAE focused on Quality Assurance Solutions/Products and Operational Excellence in delivering in a timely manner by local presence.

In 2016 expanded into Integration/Installation of RTU/FS PLC/ESD Cabinets, Wireless System for CP RMU and Photovoltaic (Solar) Systems for manufacturers like FREEWAVE Technologies, Trina Solar, Systems SUNLIGHT S.A. and The Micha Design Company.

In 2015 launched a manufacturing Industry in Ajman, UAE, with a Trade Name “A3S Solutions” under supervision of AEG Power Solutions to manufacturing of glass reinforced polyester enclosures and kiosks for application in the electrical industry.

A3S Solutions established its office in Dubai as in 2010 to cater vast Oil & Gas market in Middle East with a aim of globally aware and locally present.
